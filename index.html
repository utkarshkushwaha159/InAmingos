Who we are

Friends, by name and by nature

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), with its base in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. The name reflects the idea at its core: that real change is built on friendship — between volunteers and the communities they serve.

What began as a local effort has grown into a presence across 28 states in India, powered almost entirely by volunteers who believe in collective action. The Foundation works on the basics that every dignified life needs — food, education, livelihood, safety, and a healthy environment.

It operates as a fully registered, government-recognised non-profit, so every donation and every hour of volunteer time goes through a transparent, accountable structure.

Where the work happens

Six threads, one purpose

Every project carries its own name and its own community — colour-coded here just like the five friends in our logo.

Project SEVA

Food & clothing distribution

Weekly meal drives and clothing collections for daily-wage workers, street dwellers, and families without a safety net — because dignity starts with a full stomach and a warm layer.

Project Udaan

Women's empowerment

Vocational training, financial literacy, and livelihood skill-building that help women in underserved regions become self-reliant — a flight toward independence, one skill at a time.

Project Jeev

Animal welfare

Care, feeding, and rescue support for strays and animals in distress, run by volunteers who believe compassion shouldn't stop at the human species.

Project Prakriti

Environmental sustainability

Tree plantation drives and green-living campaigns timed around days like World Environment Day — small saplings today, a healthier earth tomorrow.

Project VIKAS

Skill Development & Livelihood

Empowering youth and communities through vocational training, employability programs, entrepreneurship support, and career guidance to build sustainable livelihoods and brighter futures.

Project Bachpanshala

Child Education

Creating joyful learning spaces for underprivileged children by providing quality education, mentorship, life skills, and opportunities that help them learn, grow, and dream beyond limitations.
